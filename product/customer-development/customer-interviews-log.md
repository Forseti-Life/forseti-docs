## Interview Tips & Reminders

### The Mom Test Principles

1. **Talk about their life, not your idea**
2. **Ask about specifics in the past, not generics or opinions about the future**
3. **Listen more than you talk**

### Good Questions ✅

- "Tell me about the last time..."
- "What do you currently do to solve this?"
- "Walk me through..."
- "How did you feel when...?"

### Bad Questions ❌

- "Would you use a product that...?" (Hypothetical)
- "Don't you think it would be great if...?" (Leading)
- "How much would you pay for...?" (Too direct, too early)

### During Interview

- [ ] Take detailed notes (or record with permission)
- [ ] Follow the script but be flexible
- [ ] Dig deeper when you hear something interesting
- [ ] Don't pitch - listen and learn
- [ ] Ask for introductions to others

### After Interview

- [ ] Send thank you email within 24 hours
- [ ] Document insights while fresh
- [ ] Add to beta list if they're interested
- [ ] Look for patterns across interviews
