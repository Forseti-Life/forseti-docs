## Lean Canvas Overview

The Lean Canvas is a 1-page business model that helps identify the riskiest parts of your business model. It's designed to be completed in 15-20 minutes and updated frequently as you learn.

**Order of completion**: Problem → Customer Segments → Unique Value Proposition → Solution → Channels → Revenue Streams → Cost Structure → Key Metrics → Unfair Advantage
