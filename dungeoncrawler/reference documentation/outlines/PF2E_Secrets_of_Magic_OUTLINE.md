## Summary
Secrets of Magic served as a comprehensive expansion of magical options for Pathfinder Second Edition. It introduced two new spellcasting classes (magus and summoner), hundreds of new spells across all magical traditions, and innovative magic item categories (fulus, grimoires, magical tattoos, spellhearts). The book deepened understanding of magical traditions through its treatises on arcane, divine, occult, and primal magic, and on the schools of magic. Chapter 5's optional subsystems allowed Game Masters to introduce specialized magical concepts like shadow magic, true names, and soulforged armaments, providing tools for creating unique magical campaign elements. The book complemented the Core Rulebook's magical foundation while expanding options for spellcasters and introducing magic-adjacent martial classes like the magus.
