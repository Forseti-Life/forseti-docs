## Summary
Guns & Gears expanded Pathfinder Second Edition with comprehensive rules for firearms and technology, introducing two new classes (inventor and gunslinger), a new ancestry (automaton), and extensive equipment options. The book was deliberately modular, allowing Game Masters to adopt the "Gears" content, the "Guns" content, or both, depending on their campaign's tone. The Rotating Gear gazetteer grounded these options in Golarion's lore while providing guidance for using technology in different regions. The book supported various play styles from Renaissance-era swashbuckling to steampunk adventure, giving players tools to create gunslingers, inventors, and technologically-minded characters.
