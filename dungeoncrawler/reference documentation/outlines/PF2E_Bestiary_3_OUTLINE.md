## Summary
Bestiary 3 continued expanding the Pathfinder Second Edition creature catalog with hundreds of additional monsters. It introduced new dragon types (Forest, Sea, Sky, Sovereign, Underworld), new creature categories (ennoisites, mortics, house spirits, tsukumogami), and expanded existing creature families. Notable additions included animated objects, clockwork constructs, and various cultural creatures from different regions of Golarion. The alphabetical organization maintained consistency with previous bestiaries while the appendix provided comprehensive reference tools for Game Masters.
