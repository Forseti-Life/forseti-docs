## Summary
Gods and Magic served as a comprehensive divine reference for Pathfinder Second Edition, focusing on deities and faith within the Inner Sea region and beyond. It expanded the religious landscape from the Core Rulebook by adding 20 major gods, 20 minor gods, numerous demigods, elemental lords, philosophical traditions, and over 100 additional deities in the appendix. The book provided both narrative context (mythology, edicts, anathema) and mechanical support (domains, spells, items, feats) for playing faithful characters, allowing players to deeply integrate their characters' beliefs into gameplay.
