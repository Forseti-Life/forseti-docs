## Summary
Bestiary 1 served as the primary creature reference for Pathfinder Second Edition, containing hundreds of monsters organized alphabetically for quick lookup. The appendix materials provided essential organizational tools, allowing Game Masters to find creatures by type, level, or special characteristics.
